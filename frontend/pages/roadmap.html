FI Financial Accounting Company code 1000 · Client 1000 · Currency USD
General Ledger
FB03
Display Document
View any FI accounting document — line items, account assignments, reference documents.
Used in: Payment Run, Cost Center, GR/IR
FS10N
G/L Account Balances
Period-by-period debit/credit/balance display for any G/L account. Key for month-end close validation.
Used in: CO-PA Board Report, GR/IR
Accounts Payable
F110
Automatic Payment Program
Manages vendor payment runs: parameters → proposal → exception review → payment posting.
Used in: Payment Run Blocked
FBL1N
Display Vendor Line Items
Open, cleared, and parked vendor items. Essential for AP reconciliation and payment block investigation.
Used in: Payment Run Blocked
MIRO
Enter Incoming Invoice
Post vendor invoices against purchase orders. Debits GR/IR account 191100, credits AP 300000.
Used in: GR/IR Mismatch
Accounts Receivable
FBL5N
Display Customer Line Items
Open AR items per customer. Use to analyse credit exposure and identify overdue invoices driving credit blocks.
Used in: Billing Credit Block
F-28
Post Incoming Payment
Post customer payments against open AR items. Clearing reduces credit exposure and can auto-release credit blocks.
Used in: Billing Credit Block
AP Payment Flow: MIROPost Invoice → FBL1NReview Open Items → F110Payment Run → FB03Verify Document
CO Controlling Controlling Area 1000 · Operating Concern 1000
Cost Center Accounting (CO-OM-CCA)
KSB1
Cost Center Actual Line Items
Individual cost postings per cost center per period with plan vs actual comparison. Primary tool for variance analysis.
Used in: IT Cost Center Overrun, CO-PA Report
Profitability Analysis (CO-PA)
KE30
Execute CO-PA Report
Profitability by segment, customer, or product. Revenue, COGS, gross profit, OpEx and EBIT margin. Used in Board reporting.
Used in: CO-PA Board Report
CO Variance Flow: KSB1Find Variance → FB03Trace Source Document → FS10NCross-check G/L → KE30Segment Impact
SD Sales & Distribution Sales Org 1000 · Distribution Channel 10 · Division 01
Sales Orders
VA03
Display Sales Order
Full sales document view: header data, items, schedule lines, delivery status, billing status, and credit status.
Used in: Billing Credit Block
VA05
List Sales Orders
List of sales orders filtered by sold-to, material, sales org, date range. Overview reporting transaction.
Billing
VF01
Create Billing Document
Creates invoice (F2), credit/debit memos from sales orders or deliveries. Fails if credit block or delivery incomplete.
Used in: Billing Credit Block
VF04
Billing Due List
List of all deliveries and orders ready for billing. Used to run collective billing at period-end.
VKM3
Blocked SD Documents
Documents blocked by credit management. Shows credit limit, current exposure, utilisation %, and block reason.
Used in: Billing Credit Block

Delivery document: picking status, goods issue status, quantities, storage location, and route.
